Culture and Heritage

Morocco is renowned for its cultural heritage, which includes ancient cities, historic sites, and traditional arts. The medieval city of Marrakech, with its bustling markets and historic palaces, is a UNESCO World Heritage site. Other notable cities include Fez, Casablanca, and Rabat, each offering a unique blend of history and modernity.

Cuisine

Moroccan cuisine is famous for its rich flavors and aromatic spices. Traditional dishes include tagine, couscous, and pastilla. The use of spices like cumin, coriander, and saffron gives Moroccan food its distinctive taste. Tea, particularly Moroccan mint tea, is a staple beverage enjoyed throughout the country.
