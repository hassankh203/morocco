Natural Wonders

The natural landscapes of Morocco are equally impressive. The Sahara Desert, the Atlas Mountains, and the beautiful beaches along the coast provide a range of outdoor activities and stunning views. The country is also home to diverse wildlife, including various species of birds and mammals.

Top Tourist Attractions

  • Jemaa el-Fnaa, Marrakech
  • Hassan II Mosque, Casablanca
  • Ait Ben Haddou
  • Fez Medina
  • Erg Chebbi, Sahara Desert
